Cell Warfare About

Instructions

First pick how you want the map to look like, to do that, press W, A, S or D on the keyboard and when you have choosen, press ENTER to start the game.

At the beginning of the game, the two teams receive a random cell, colonized with 3 fortification points and +3 money.

The game is turn based, a turn lasts between 3-6 seconds and at the end of a turn, you receive +1 money for each cell colonized.

Money can be used for:

  • - Dealing 1 damage to a enemy cell (only once per turn)
  • - Colonizing a uncolonized cell with 1 fortification
  • - Add 1 fortitication to a friendly colonized cell.

When a cell's fortification comes to 0, it becomes uncolozined.

The Kamikase button: when pressed, takes 1 fortification off out of every colonized cell of the game.

Your taken tiles are blue (and get more blue every for incrementing fortification), red for the enemy (and also gets darker the more fortification)

Game ends when one player has all

A display on screen pulses when a new turn starts, turn duration is random to make decisions about money spending more dinamic.
