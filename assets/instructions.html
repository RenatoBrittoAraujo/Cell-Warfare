Instructions

First pick how you want the map to look like, to do that, press W, A, S or D on the keyboard and when you have choosen, press ENTER to start the game.

At the beginning of the game, the two teams receive a random cell, colonized with 3 fortification points and +3 money.

Each turn lasts 3 seconds, at the end of a turn, you receive +1 money for each cell colonized.

Money can be used for: 1) Dealing 1 damage to a enemy cell (only once per turn); 2) Colonizing a uncolonized cell with 1 fortification; 3) Add 1 fortitication to a friendly colonized cell.

When a cell's fortification becomes 0, it becomes uncolozined.

The Kamikase button: when pressed, takes 1 fortification off out of every colonized cell of the game.
